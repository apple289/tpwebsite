THE WHO

Hello there, I’m Razvan Garofeanu, the self-taught, not so famous UI / UX / Web & Graphic Designer, founder of Garphee and co-founder of Templatypus. I’m a Public Relations graduate who’s making a living out of advertising. Currently i live in Iasi/Romania and i have enjoyed doing multidisciplinary design for more than 7 years now. I don’t code... Yeah, i know. I use the Adobe Master Collection Suite for my projects and i often space out thinking about innovation. I don’t enjoy rushing into a project, because I believe the final product should make both the client and I proud. I choose the projects i work on and i’m responsible for their well-being from start to finish. Wanna do a project together ? Poke me!
